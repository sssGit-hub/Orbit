🎯 NEWTON'S CANNON
A thought experiment from 1687
Sir Isaac Newton imagined firing a cannonball horizontally from a mountaintop. If it's too slow, it falls back to Earth. At just the right speed, it falls around Earth forever — that's orbit. Faster still, and it escapes gravity entirely.
